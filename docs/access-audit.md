## How integrations access work in Notion

A PAT-backed integration (here, `notion-labs`) can only see a page or database if **both** are true:

1. The **teamspace** that contains it allows integrations (teamspace setting,
   controlled by teamspace owners).
2. The specific page/database has the integration added as a **connection**
   (`•••` → Connections → add the integration).

If a teamspace has integrations disabled, **nothing inside it** can be shared
with the integration — including all its sub-pages and databases.

## The Ecosystem teamspace is blocked at the teamspace level

The **Ecosystem** teamspace cannot have `notion-labs` added to it. This is a
teamspace-level restriction, so the entire teamspace is unreachable by the PAT
/ `ntn`, regardless of per-page sharing:

- 🧩 Ecosystem hub
- 🏢 Company (and children: One Pagers, Business Model Canvas, Legal
  Agreements, Ecosystem Team, Job Descriptions Inspiration, AGIV — LinkedIn
  Growth, Merch, Late Night Coffee Shop)
- 🤝 Community
- 🎪 Initiatives & Programs (Ecosystem Projects, Hacker House, Accelerator,
  Sponsorship, UGC Program, Ecosystem Initiatives, Events DB, View of Events)
- 📇 Contacts & CRM (Co-organizer Contacts, CRM for Universities, Vendors DB)
- 📝 Notes & Postmortems (Ecosystem Notes DB)
- 🗂️ Archive

> Note: some databases may exist in **both** the Ecosystem teamspace and the
> Applied AI/CRM area (e.g. Meetings). The row below reflects the specific
> database ID in `docs/data-map.md`. If a database physically lives in
> Ecosystem, it is blocked by the teamspace rule even if it appears elsewhere.
